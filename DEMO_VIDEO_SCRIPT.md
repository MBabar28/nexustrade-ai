## Recording Tips

1. Use a screen recorder (OBS, Loom, or QuickTime)
2. Set browser to full screen, hide bookmarks bar
3. Zoom to 110-125% so text is readable
4. Navigate slowly between tabs — give viewers time to read
5. Place at least one paper trade live to show the flow
6. Run a signal scan and Monte Carlo simulation on camera
7. Keep mouse movements smooth and deliberate
8. Record voiceover separately if possible — cleaner audio
9. Add subtle background music (lo-fi or ambient)
10. Export at 1080p, under 50MB for easy Twitter upload
